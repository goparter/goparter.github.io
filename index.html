我们的故事Our Story

2009年，毕业季，一个机缘巧合下我们进入了制药领域的行业，而这一投入就是十年。 十年里我们经历了从GMP到2010版GMP再到飞检等各项政策的变革；从硬件、软件、人员、新技术的要求提升。 无论是作为供应商，还是企业本身，都清楚的认识到，在GMP实施过程中，人员是主导因素，也是最大的污染源。 同时对于食药品生产，需要对人员的更衣、操作技术及接受的培训进行有效管理。 2018年，一群热爱这个行业的我们走到了一起，好搭档（GoodPartner)便孕育而生。 我们怀揣梦想，也有一腔热血，我们愿意为这个行业奋斗一生。 为了加强对食药品生产过程的管理，我们根据2010版新版GMP及“无菌药品附录”的基础上，考察了FDA、EMEA、WHO、ISPE、ICH等相关的技术文件，同时参考了国内外多家知名制药企业成熟有效的管理经验，针对无菌区、洁净区人员行为规范的管理规定、人体防护产品的着装材料、款式要求，设计出即符合GMP法规要求，又符合人体学的整套人体特种防护用品。经后我们将继续坚持“专注专业， 科学合理”的经营理念；生产符合GMP法规要求产品的同时，也将不断创新设计理念，提供有效合理的更衣配置方案，从根本控制风险源头，避免过度防护，降低用户使用成本。

In the graduation season of 2009, we entered the pharmaceutical industry by an occasional opportunity, and this investment lasted for ten years. In the past ten years, we have experienced various policy changes from GMP to 2010 version of GMP to flight inspection, and the requirements of hardware, software, personnel and new technology have been upgraded. Whether as a supplier or an enterprise itself, it is clear that in the process of GMP implementation, people are the leading factor but the biggest source of pollution. At the same time, for the production of food and drug, it is necessary to manage the personnel's changing clothes effectively, operation technology and training. In 2018, a group of people who love this industry came together and a GoodPartner was born. We have a dream, and we also have a cavity of blood, we are willing to fight for this industry for a lifetime. In order to strengthen the management of food and drug production process, we inspected the technical documents of FDA, EMEA, WHO, ISPE and ICH based on the new version of GMP and "Appendix to Sterile Drugs" in 2010. At the same time, we consulted the mature and effective management experience of many well-known pharmaceutical enterprises at home and abroad, and according to the management regulations of personnel's behavior standards in sterile and clean clean areas as well as the requirements of dressing material and styles, a set of special protective articles for human body is designed, which not only meets the requirements of GMP regulations, but also meets the requirements of anthropology. After that, we will continue to adhere to the "professional, scientific and rational" business philosophy; while producing products that meet the requirements of GMP regulations, we will also continue to innovate design concepts, provide effective and reasonable dressing configuration scheme, control the sources of risk fundamentally, avoid excessive protection, and reduce user use costs.
